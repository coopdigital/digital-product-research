Energy demand has huge peaks and troughs so energy is much more expensive and a lower percentage is renewable at peak times . Domestic users’ rates assume that you’re going to use energy at the peak times.

Smart meters are being installed in everyone’s houses which will open up opportunities for more dynamic pricing for consumers based on actual usage.

Would people allow us to take control of their fridges to adjust demand away from the peak periods?
