The generation of renewable energy is hard to forecast and, in the case of solar, does not align with peak demand times.

If we show people what times the energy in their homes is going to be coming from non-renewable sources for that day, would they change their behaviour?

We built a prototype of a carbon clock which shows the expected percentage of carbon fuels for the next 24 hours using a traffic light system.
